About the Kansas City Perl Mongers

The Kansas City Perl Mongers consists of a diverse group of individuals with a unifying interest in expanding our knowledge of Perl. We encourage participation by individuals of all skill levels relating to Perl.

We meet on the second Tuesday of the month from 7-9 PM. The main mode of communication is by way of the mailing list.

To learn more about the Perl Mongers organization please visit: http://www.perlmongers.org/
