Upcoming Meetings

Meetings are generally the second Tuesday of each month at 7pm. We currently meet at Barley's Brewhaus in Shawnee at 16649 Midland Drive, the southeast corner of Midland and Renner, just east of I-435. There's a wider variety of food than at our previous meeting location, and they serve beer. Underage perl mongers are still allowed in, though, so don't let that keep you away. Just look for us in the back, by the pool tables. We'll have a couple of hightop tables pushed together.

New members are always welcome. Any potential attendees are encouraged to check the mailing list to see if we're really meeting and to encourage those of us who are forgetful.
